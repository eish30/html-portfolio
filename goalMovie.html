The Best Movies according to Eishani

My top 3 movies of all time...

The Chronicles of Narnia: The Lion, The Witch and The Wardrobe

A movie about the magical world of Narnia locked inside a wardrobe.

Elemental

A love story of a fire girl and a water boy in the city of natural elements.

Soulmates

A story about two best friends depicting how even friends and not just lovers can be soulmates.
